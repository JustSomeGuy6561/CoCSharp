
Regex to match braces. paste this after some function header and it'll match all the braces for it
\r?{(?>{(?<c>)|[^{}]+|}(?<-c>))*(?(c)(?!))}

Same, but for parenthesis
\r?\((?>\((?<c>)|[^()]+|\)(?<-c>))*(?(c)(?!))\)
